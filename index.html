Our Story

Rooted in passion, grown with care

Bloom & Petal was born from a simple belief — that flowers have the power to transform moments into memories. Founded in 2008 in the heart of Vancouver, we've spent over 15 years perfecting the art of floral arrangement.

Every bouquet we create is a labour of love. We source the freshest stems each morning, working with local growers and trusted international partners to ensure that every flower that leaves our shop is at its most beautiful.
